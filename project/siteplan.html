Delightfully Balanced Site Plan

Site Name

Delightfully Balanced - Is a simple name that show the idea of a healthy eating and reprensent the main idea of the site. domain: delightfullybalanced.co

Site Purpose

This site give some of healthy recipes, cooking tips, and nutritional advices for people who want to improve their habits with the food. It also have a meal plans and lists of ingredients for a balanced eating way.

Scenarios

  • What are some healthy meal recipes that i can cook around 30 minutes?
  • Where can I find a plan for a balanced diet?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) – Used for headers and buttons.
  • Secondary Color: #d9e9e0 (Light Background) – Used for background and sections.

Typography

  • Font 1- Winky Sans – used for body text.
  • Font 2- Exo 2 – used for headings to give a classy look.
